These are some of our favourite Land Cruiser fanatics. They are generally very well known in the 4×4 and Land Cruiser communities for their informative and ‘salt of the earth’ perspectives and opinions on their passions.

## Johan Badenhorst

![Johan Badenhorst — Voetspore presenter and South African overlanding icon](https://pub-6c900fb2e73a4b89bc049099101e4591.r2.dev/images/posts/our-favourite-land-cruiser-fanatics-2.jpg)

Badenhorst is an icon in the South African 4×4 and overlanding communities. He is particularly well known for his lead role in the famous Afrikaans TV show, Voetspoere . Johan knows a thing or two about Land Cruisers, and we’ve always enjoyed listening to and watching him on TV.  His adventures and programmes have captivated a wide range of South African audiences for a long time now.

[voetspore.co.za](https://www.voetspore.co.za)

## Andrew St Pierre White

![Andrew St Pierre White — world-renowned 4x4 writer, commentator and film maker](https://pub-6c900fb2e73a4b89bc049099101e4591.r2.dev/images/posts/our-favourite-land-cruiser-fanatics-3.jpg)

One of the world’s best known 4×4 writers, commentators and adventure-travel film makers. He first made his mark in the 4×4 overland community in 1993 as the author of The Complete Guide to Four-Wheel Drive in Southern Africa, and has been rolling ever since. We thoroughly enjoy watching him whether it’s bouncing along the Makgadikgadi Pans in Botswana, or traversing the Australian Outback.

[4xoverland.com](https://www.4xoverland.com)

## Paul Marsh

![Paul Marsh — overlanding expedition expert and 4x4 trainer](https://pub-6c900fb2e73a4b89bc049099101e4591.r2.dev/images/posts/our-favourite-land-cruiser-fanatics-4.jpg)

Paul’s passion for the great outdoors and overland travel means he has a vast experience in all things overlanding, and leaves no detail out when it comes to expedition planning. This can be seen in the meticulous approach he takes in vehicle preparation, as well as training and support of his clients. His engineering background only compliments this. Paul’s purpose is to enable people from all over the world to fulfill their dream of overland travel in their own 4×4.

[paulmarsh4x4.com](https://www.paulmarsh4x4.com)

## Conclusion

And so there you have it, a couple of our favourite Land Cruiser fanatics. People we have come to listen to and respect in their opinions and perspectives of overlanding and 4x4ing in a Land Cruiser. And while there are many more very well versed Land Cruiser enthusiasts out there, these make it to the top of our list.
