Partnership Terms

  1. Deliverables LandCruiserSA will display the Advertiser's banner on every vehicle listing page and maintain a Featured Partner listing, for the duration of the term. Placements click through to the Advertiser-nominated URL. Content must align with LandCruiserSA's brand and audience; LandCruiserSA may reject or request modification of any creative.
  2. Creative Supplied by the Advertiser in two sizes — 728×90 leaderboard and 300×250 rectangle, PNG/JPG at 2× resolution (1456×180 / 600×500). LandCruiserSA may scale or crop for responsive display but will not alter brand elements. Placements go live within 48 hours of receiving approved creative.
  3. Attribution & commission A "converted sale" is any sale — online, telephonic or in-person — where code LCSA10 is quoted or redeemed. The Advertiser will keep reasonable records of code usage and report the monthly count by the 5th of the following month. Commission is invoiced monthly in arrears against that count.
  4. Reporting & review LandCruiserSA provides monthly banner impressions and click counts (first-party tracking). Both parties will review the shared numbers together in week 12 of the trial to agree the ongoing rate and term. LandCruiserSA provides audience insights but does not guarantee specific results.
  5. Payment The retainer is invoiced monthly in advance and payable within 7 days. Late payment may result in placement suspension. No refunds once a month's placement is live.
  6. Term & termination The trial runs 3 months from go-live. Thereafter the arrangement continues month-to-month; either party may terminate on 14 days' written notice. Accrued commission remains payable on termination.
  7. Independence & exclusivity Editorial content on landcruisersa.co.za remains independent of the Advertiser; placements are clearly commercial. This arrangement is non-exclusive for both parties.
  8. Liability LandCruiserSA is not responsible for claims, damages or losses arising from the Advertiser's products or advertisements. The Advertiser is responsible for the legal and regulatory compliance of its advertising material.
  9. Data privacy (POPIA) No personal customer data is exchanged under this agreement — attribution runs on the discount code and aggregate counts only. LandCruiserSA processes personal information in accordance with POPIA; see the Privacy Policy and PAIA Manual at landcruisersa.co.za/privacy-policy/. Queries: info@landcruisersa.co.za.
