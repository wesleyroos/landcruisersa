1 · Purpose

The Spotter operates landcruisersa.co.za and will refer prospective vehicle buyers (persons enquiring about the Dealer's stock) and, where applicable, prospective sellers (private owners whose vehicle the Dealer may wish to purchase) to the Dealer, in exchange for the success fees in Schedule A.

2 · Referral mechanics

  1. Every referral is first captured by the Spotter and forwarded to the Dealer with a unique reference number (format LCSA-R-YYYY-NNNN) identifying the lead, the vehicle of interest, and the date.
  2. A lead is a Qualified Referral only if introduced under a reference number. Persons already recorded in the Dealer's CRM at the time of referral are excluded — the Dealer must flag such exclusions within 5 business days of receiving the referral, failing which the referral stands.
  3. A fee is earned when a Qualified Referral completes a purchase from the Dealer (buyer-side) or when the Dealer purchases the referred vehicle (seller-side), within 90 days of referral.

3 · Reporting & audit

  1. The Dealer reports the outcome of each open referral (sold / lost / in progress) monthly, by reference number.
  2. The Spotter may contact referred persons directly to confirm outcomes. Referred persons are informed of the referral relationship.
  3. On reasonable notice, the Spotter may request supporting confirmation (e.g. invoice date, not price) for any referral reported as sold or lost.

4 · Payment

Fees are invoiced when a sale is confirmed and payable within 14 days of invoice. Late amounts bear interest at the prime overdraft rate plus 2%.

5 · Relationship, non-exclusivity & independence

  1. This is a non-exclusive arrangement. Both parties remain free to work with any other party, and the Spotter's website remains an independent, whole-of-market platform — nothing in this agreement obliges the Spotter to feature, rank or promote the Dealer's stock.
  2. The Spotter acts as an independent referrer — not an agent, employee, partner or F&I intermediary of the Dealer. The Dealer alone concludes all sales and is responsible for its own regulatory compliance (incl. CPA and FICA).

6 · Personal information (POPIA)

Referred persons consent to their details being shared for the referred purpose. The Dealer may use referred details only to serve that enquiry and must not add them to marketing lists without the person's own consent.

7 · Term & termination

Month-to-month from the agreement date. Either party may terminate on 30 days' written notice (email suffices). Fees on referrals made before termination remain payable if the sale completes within the 90-day window.
