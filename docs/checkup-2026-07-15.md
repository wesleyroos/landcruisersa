The data validates the reframe rather than challenging it:
1. **Consumer funnel converts weakly** (0 finance leads, 3.9% cert capture,
   4 signups) while **audience assets compound** (IG hero engine, game-viewers,
   ChatGPT 8.6×, handoffs +19%). Exactly the "data-and-relationships business
   with a consumer site" thesis.
2. **Every rand-shaped thread is a relationship** — Titan (FIL intro), WBC
   (Tersia), Blair (inbound), Gys (in person). The consumer funnel produced
   traffic, not revenue. Traction scoreboard after month 1: **still R0, 0
   signed** — the constraint is closing, not building.

   game-viewers→#1.4 in days are the same motion: find weak SERP / real demand,
   ship the citable structured answer, attach a partner. That's the WBC pitch,
   now with two fresh proof points.

**The one thing to change:** nothing structural — close a deal. Titan is
closest (proposal due today), D1 WBC kill-or-advance is Friday 18-Jul, D6 Blair
24-Jul. The build side can idle for a week without cost; the deal side can't.
